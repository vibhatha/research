## Board and Member Meetings in the Tourism Sector

The sources, specifically the **Tourism Act, No. 38 of 2005**, detail the protocols for meetings and member conduct for the various boards and committees under the tourism portfolio.

### **Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority (SLTDA)**
*   **Presiding Officer:** The Chairman is required to preside at all meetings. In their absence, the members present elect one of their own to preside.
*   **Voting:** The Chairman or the person presiding holds an original vote and a **casting vote** in the event of a tie.
*   **Quorum:** The quorum for any meeting of the Authority is **five members**, unless the Authority agrees otherwise.
*   **Rules of Procedure:** The Authority is empowered to make its own rules regarding the procedures followed at meetings and the transaction of business.
*   **Conflict of Interest:** If a member has a direct or indirect interest in a contract entered into or proposed by the Authority, they must **disclose the nature of that interest** at a meeting. This disclosure must be recorded in the minutes, and the member is prohibited from participating in any deliberation or decision regarding that matter.
*   **Absence:** A member is disqualified from their position if they are absent from **three consecutive meetings** without prior notice.

### **Sri Lanka Convention Bureau (SLCB)**
*   **Attendance and Removal:** The Board of Management of the Convention Bureau has the power to remove any member who is absent from **three consecutive board meetings** without being represented by an alternate member.
*   **Conflict of Interest:** A member is strictly prohibited from voting on any contract in which they are directly interested.
*   **Alternate Members:** These individuals are entitled to receive notices of all meetings. They may attend and vote as a member only if the person who appointed them is not present.

### **Sri Lanka Institute of Tourism and Hotel Management (SLITHM)**
*   The **Board of Management** of the Institute is charged with its management and administration.
*   **Remuneration:** Members of the Board and other officers may be paid remuneration out of the fund as determined by the Authority in consultation with the Minister in charge of the subject of Finance.

### **General Provisions and Advisory Committee**
*   **Validity of Proceedings:** No act or proceeding of these bodies is deemed invalid solely because of a vacancy in membership or a defect in the appointment of a member.
*   **Ministerial Directions:** The Minister has the authority to issue general or special directions in writing to the **Tourism Advisory Committee** regarding the performance of its duties, and the committee must give effect to these directions.
